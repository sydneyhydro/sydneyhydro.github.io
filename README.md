# Sydney Hydrology Day 2026

Official website for Sydney Hydrology Day — an annual one-day forum bringing together researchers and professionals across Sydney to share advances in hydrology and water science.
The first Sydney Hydrology Day took place on 10 February 2026.

**Website:** [https://sydneyhydro.github.io](https://sydneyhydro.github.io)

- **Venue:** The University of Sydney, TBA

## About

Sydney Hydrology Day has a focus on Water Scienc in general. The day blends panel discussions and themed presentation sessions to highlight current research, practical applications, and emerging methods in hydrology. With a strong focus on postgraduate and early-career work, the day fosters collaboration between universities and industry.
