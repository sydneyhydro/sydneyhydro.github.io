About the Event

Sydney Hydrology Day is an annual one-day forum that brings together researchers and professionals across Sydney to share advances in hydrology. The program blends panel discussion and themed presentation sessions to highlight current research, practical applications, and emerging methods. With a strong focus on postgraduate and early‑career work, the day fosters collaboration between universities and industry and strengthens the local hydrology community.


This year’s event will be taking place on 10 February 2026.
